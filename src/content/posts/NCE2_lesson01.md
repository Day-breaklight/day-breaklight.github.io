## Lesson 1 A private conversation
### Words and Expressions
private adj. 私人的  
conversation n. 谈话  
theatre n. 剧场，戏院  
seat n. 座位  
play n. 戏  
loudiy adv. 大声地  
angry adj. 生气的  
angrily adv. 生气地  
attention n. 注意  
bear（bore，borne） v. 容忍  
business n. 事  
rudely adv. 无理地、粗鲁地  
### Note on the text
go the theatre, 去看戏。  
got angry, 生气。  
turn round，转身，也可用turn around。  
pay attention, 注意。  
I could not bear it. 我无法忍受。其中的it是指上文中的那对男女大声说话又不理会作者的愤怒目光。  
none of your business, 不关你的事。  
### Further notes on the text  
1. **Last week I went to the theatre. 上个礼拜我去了戏院。**  
go to the theatre 去看戏  
定冠词 the 表示去某一场所的一种特定活动  
**e.g.**  
go to the cinema/show  
go to the bank/post office  
2. **I did not enjoy it (the play). 我没法欣赏这个戏。**  
**e.g.**  
I did not enjoy the meal/book.  
3. **I got very angry. 我很愤怒。**  
got，指“变得怎么样”，相当于 be 动词，是一个连系动词。  
**e.g.**  
The food is getting cold.  
4. **I could not hear the actors. 我没法听见演员说什么。**  
hear somebody，听见某人说什么，hear是听见，表示“听”的行为本身要用listen。  
**e.g.**  
Do you hear me?  
He`s not listening to me.  

5. **I looked at the young man and young woman angrily. 我怒视着那一男一女。**  
“看”有不同的表达方法，look at 看谁，强调“看”的动作，也可说 see，看见。  
watch 表示“观看”的意思  
**e.g.**  
Did you see Sam yeaterday?  
She`s watching TV.  
6. **They did not pay any attention.(to me) 他们丝毫没有理会我。**  
pay attention 注意，后跟宾语要加介词 to  
**e.g.**  
Please pay attention to that picture on the wall.  
7. **In the end, T could not bear it. 最后，我实在是无法容忍了。**  
**（it 指代前面的情形）**  
in the end = at last

### Grammar in use  
**一般过去时和过去进行时**  
**一般过去时**  
一般过去时（Simple Past Tense）是英语中用来描述**s在过去某个时间发生并已经结束的动作或状态**的时态。它常用于讲述故事、回忆过去的事情，或者说明过去某个时间点发生的事实。  
一、基本用法  
表示过去某一时间发生的动作或存在的状态  
I visited my grandparents last weekend.  
（我上周末去看望了祖父母。）  
She was a teacher in 2015.  
（她在2015年是一名老师。）  
表示过去习惯性的动作（相当于“used to”）  
When I was a child, I played football every day.  
（我小时候每天都踢足球。）  
在时间状语从句中，表示“刚刚……就……”等含义（常与 when, after, before 等连用）  
When he arrived, we left.  
（他一到，我们就走了。）  
二、构成方式  
1. 肯定句  
规则动词：动词原形 + -ed  
work → worked  
play → played  
不规则动词：需单独记忆（如 go → went, eat → ate, see → saw）  
I saw a movie yesterday.  
be 动词：  
主语是 I / he / she / it → was  
主语是 you / we / they → were  
2. 否定句  
实义动词：did not (didn't) + 动词原形  
He didn’t go to school yesterday.  
be 动词：直接在 was/were 后加 not  
She wasn’t at home last night.  
3. 疑问句  
实义动词：Did + 主语 + 动词原形？  
Did you call me last night?  
be 动词：Was/Were + 主语...?  
Were they happy with the result?  
三、常见时间状语  
一般过去时常与以下时间状语连用：  
yesterday（昨天）  
last week/month/year（上周/上个月/去年）  
in 2010（在2010年）  
two days ago（两天前）  
when I was young（当我年轻的时候）  
**e.g.**  
I had a very good seat.  
The play was interesting.  
They did not pay any attention.  
I could not hear the actors.  
**过去进行时**  
A young man and a young woman were sitting behind me.  
They were talking loudly.
